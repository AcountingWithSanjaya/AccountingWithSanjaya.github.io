Digital Credits Return Policy

No Returns

Our No-Return Policy for Digital Credits

At Quiver, we provide digital credits and subscription services. Due to the instant-access nature of our digital products, we do not accept returns or provide refunds under any circumstances.

All credit purchases and subscription activations are final once processed.

Important Notice

By completing a credit purchase or subscription activation, you acknowledge and agree that these digital services are non-refundable.

Why Credits Cannot Be Refunded

  • Instant Activation

    Credits are activated instantly upon purchase and immediately available for use.

  • Digital Nature

    As a digital product, credits cannot be "returned" once they are added to your account.

  • Account Security

    To maintain account integrity and prevent abuse, all credit transactions are final.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I purchased the wrong credit package?

All credit purchases are final. Please carefully review your selection before confirming the purchase. If you need guidance on which credit package best suits your needs, contact our support team before making a purchase.

Can I transfer my credits to another account?

Credits are non-transferable and tied to the account they were purchased for. They cannot be moved between accounts or converted to other forms of currency.

What happens to unused credits?

Credits remain valid in your account until used. They do not expire, but they cannot be refunded or exchanged for monetary value.

What about subscription cancellations?

While you can cancel a subscription at any time, you will continue to have access until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial subscription periods.

Need Assistance?

If you have questions about our credit system or need help choosing the right package, please contact our support team before making a purchase.
